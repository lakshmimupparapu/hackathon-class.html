NavGurukul

Founded by IIT Delhi and Harvard alumni, NavGurukul is a non-government organization that has been making a transformative impact for over 7 years. We're dedicated to empowering underserved communities through education and skills development, opening doors to desirable jobs. Our one-year residential courses provide students with a Certificate in Software Programming. We're proud to have achieved 630+ success stories and an impressive earning of INR 16 crore annually by our alumni. With over 1,000 students learning in our residential centres across Bangalore, Pune, Delhi, Tripura, Amravati, and Dharamsala, we're continuously expanding our reach.

Nidhi Anarkat-Co-Founder and CEO-NavGuruku

Why do you want to join NavGurukul?
It enables them to aspire for the same jobs and live a life of similar possibilities as people from the top of the pyramid. NavGurukul stands for an equitable higher education alternative where students learn the right skills, mindsets, and the ability to learn on their own to thrive in the world.
What is the NavGurukul Code India Fellowship? Started in 2016, Navgurukul runs one-year residential course for students from underserved and diverse communities. Students after pursuing our course are awarded Certificate in Software Programming.

The residential program is open for students who have finished just 10th grade and are 17 years of age. We are expanding our offering to digital careers other than programming, such as design and management
